My Story

The Search

As a practicing lawyer in Vancouver, I spent years searching for a time tracking tool that actually understood the reality of legal work. Every solution I found was either too complex, too simplistic, or required constant manual input that disrupted my workflow. I tried dozens of tools, but nothing captured the nuances of legal practice the interrupted phone calls, the quick client emails, the research that spans multiple matters.

The Breakthrough

Then AI technology evolved to a point where I realized: I could finally build the tool I'd been searching for. AI could understand context, learn patterns, and automate the tedious parts of time tracking while maintaining the accuracy and detail that legal billing demands. What seemed impossible just a few years ago became not only possible but practical.

The Solution

TimeLawg is the result of that journey. It's built by someone who understands the daily challenges of legal practice because I live them. It's designed to capture every billable moment without interrupting your work. It learns your patterns, understands your matters, and creates accurate time entries automatically. This is the tool I needed and now it exists.
